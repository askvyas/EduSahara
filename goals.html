Goals

'Practice makes a man perfect' is a very old saying. Practice is inevitable in sports and education, especially in problem solving subjects like mathematics. EduSahara™ has its roots in the requirement that the student should have more practice to become familiar with the concepts and pickup speed in solving.
EduSahara™ aims to fulfill three expectations of the student :

  • Continuous practice by posing the same problem with different data irons out all common mistakes that the student normally makes while solving problems This applies to simple addition and subtraction of integers and fractions all the way to complex problems in algebra and geometry.
  • Continuous practice improves the calculation speed of the student.
  • Exposure to a variety of problems posed with relevance to the concepts taught gives correct understanding of the concepts, improves the thought process and brings confidence to the student
