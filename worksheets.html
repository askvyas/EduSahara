EduSahara™ Sample Worksheets

Here is a small sample of what EduSahara™ is going to offer you. The following worksheets are generated using Teacher Edition of Edusahara™. You can use these worksheets for your personal (non-commercial) use. You can get a first hand feel of the kind of questions generated by the software. This is only a part of the whole. In other editions, these assignments can be answered within the software and pdf files cannot be generated. A record of all your assignment activity is stored in EduSahara™. The answers to the questions in the worksheet are at the end of the worksheet. One great feature of the software is that every time you generate the assignment, even though the questions are same, they appear with different data. This gives a great opportunity to the students to practice and move them away from stereotype Rote Learning of the steps.

Any issues that you see in these worksheets can be reported to support@edusahara.com.
